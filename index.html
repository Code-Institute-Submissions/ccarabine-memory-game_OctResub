Rules


How to play the Memory game?


Just click on "Start New Game" to start the game.


The purpose of this memory game:


To memorize the locations of the cards in the game in the least moves and to make pairs of cards by turning them over 2 by 2. When the 2 cards match, it's a pair! You win the pair and the cards remain turned face up. You will then continue the game, otherwise the cards are automatically turned face down and you have to make a new try until you have found the 6 pairs of cards. You will be given a final score which will depend on the number of moves and the bonus questions answered


Scoring


The final score is calculated from the number of moves taken and bonus questions

  • Less than 7 moves, the player receives 50 points
  • Less than 10 moves, the player receives 40 points
  • Less than 13 moves, the player receives 30 points
  • Less than 16 moves, the player receives 20 points
  • Less than 19 moves, the player receives 10 points
  • More then 20 moves, the player receives zero points
  • Bonus question answered correctly, the player receives 15 points

Every time you start a new game, a random selection and a random positioning of the cards ensure you a different game than the previous one, so you can play as many times as you want!
